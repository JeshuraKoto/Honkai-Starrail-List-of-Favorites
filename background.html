Background: Kafka & Blade

Kafka

Kafka is a key member of the Stellaron Hunters, known for her calm demeanor and strategic mind. She manipulates enemies using psychological tactics and lightning-based attacks.

Blade

Blade is a cursed swordsman who cannot die, wielding a self-destructive fighting style. His mysterious past connects him to Kafka and the Stellaron Hunters, leading to his current fate.
